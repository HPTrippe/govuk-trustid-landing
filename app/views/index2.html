Tell us what identity checks your service does

The GDS Digital Identity programme wants to make it easier for teams across government to check who their users are when they access services.

We're designing a new service to help teams do these checks. To make sure we build the right thing, we want to talk to service teams from different departments and agencies. If you work on one of these teams and are involved in making sure only the right users have access to your service, then we'd like to talk to you.

Get in touch

What we want to find out

We want to find out how you check who your users are when they access your service. You might currently do this by:

  • proving their identities
  • checking or collecting information to make sure they're eligible

We'd like to know how you decided what checks you should do. We also want to know more about what the checks involve and any existing products or components you use to do them.
